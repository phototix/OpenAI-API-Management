Privacy

API Key Dashboard respects your privacy and is designed to keep sensitive data on your device by default.

Data Storage

  • Local by default: Your accounts and API keys are stored in your browser's local storage.
  • No automatic sync: Nothing is sent to any server unless you explicitly use the optional backup feature.

Optional Cloud Backup

You may opt to back up your local data via MasterAuth hosted at n8n.brandon.my. When you press Upload, your local data (including API keys) is encrypted with a password-derived key and sent to the backup service. Download replaces local data with your cloud backup.

Network Calls

  • Direct provider APIs: The app calls provider endpoints (OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, xAI Grok, Google Billing) only when you choose to refresh balances.
  • No tracking pixels: No analytics or ad trackers are embedded.

Affiliation

This project is independent and not affiliated with OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI, Google, or DeepSeek.
